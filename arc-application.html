What you'll be screaming about

Stephanie

What the three-legged table!

Waking up in bed next to a stranger is bad. Waking up next to Cooper Mallory is catastrophic. Yet here we are. Several canceled flights and one wrong bedroom will throw even the best-laid plans out the window.

Mallory—with his double-dimpled, all-American, good-guy charm—is exactly the kind of man I need to keep my distance from. I’ve kept a secret about him for eighteen months. I’ve been burned by men like him before. Unfortunately, my traitorous feelings refuse to be reasonable about it.

But I think it’s going to be fine. Everything is fine. Right?

Cooper

I have exactly zero idea why any of this is happening.

I’ve spent eighteen months trying everything to get on Stephanie’s good side, and she keeps me at arm’s length. She’s funny, fierce, and the most magnetic person I’ve ever met—but she treats me like a mildly aggressive disease. I cannot figure out what I did wrong.

The worst part? Even after months of being her least-favorite person in any given room, I’m still all in. Make that make sense.

A secret that’s bound to detonate, unresolved tension with nowhere to go, and chemistry that absolutely refuses to behave—it all starts a chain reaction that nobody sees coming and absolutely nobody can stop.

The truth is going to come out eventually. The only question is what will happen when it does.
